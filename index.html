How to Play

  • Slide Flower Tiles: Move identical tiles into each other along open paths.

  • Use Helpful Tools: Tap the hint, shuffle, or shovel if you're ever stuck.

  • Clear the Board: Make strategic matches to remove all tiles.

  • Perfect the Garden: Earn stars by completing levels with minimal moves.

  • Unlock New Themes: Progress through levels to access new tile visuals and floral settings.
